Privacy Policy

SciencefactPal

SciencefactPal ("the app") is a personal tool that generates short educational science videos and publishes them to the owner's own social media accounts.

Data we use

When you connect your TikTok account, the app receives an OAuth access token and refresh token. These are stored locally on the owner's computer and used solely to upload and publish videos to your own account. The app does not read your personal data, your videos, your followers, or any other account.

Data we do not collect

We do not collect, sell, share, or transmit personal information to third parties. No analytics or tracking.

Storage

Tokens are stored locally and can be deleted at any time by removing the local credentials file or revoking access in your TikTok settings.
